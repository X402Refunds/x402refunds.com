- "Your agents, your rules, your control - always"
- National override on ANY supranational decision
- Complete data sovereignty (agents stored in national jurisdiction)
- Zero backdoors or external control mechanisms

**💼 Economic Benefits:**
- "Be the first to govern the AI agent economy in your jurisdiction"
- Create national competitive advantages in AI governance
- Generate revenue from agent registration fees and dispute resolution
- Attract international AI companies seeking clear governance

**🔐 National Security:**
- Full visibility into all AI agents operating in national territory  
- Ability to instantly restrict or expel foreign agents
- Integration with existing national security frameworks
- Emergency shutdown capabilities for national crises

### **UN Value Proposition: "Coordinator, Not Controller"**

**🌐 UN's Role (When Opted-In):**
```
📋 FACILITATION LAYER
├── Best Practice Sharing Between Nations
├── International Dispute Mediation (Advisory Only)
├── Global AI Agent Standards Development  
├── Cross-Border Coordination Protocols

🤝 COORDINATION SERVICES
├── Treaty Template Library for Bilateral Agreements
├── Technical Standards for Interoperability
├── Diplomatic Channel for Agent-Related Disputes
└── Research & Development Coordination

⚖️ ADVISORY JURISDICTION
├── International Agent Court (Appeals Only)  
├── Cross-Border Evidence Sharing Protocols
├── Global Agent Crime Coordination (Terrorism, etc.)
└── Humanitarian Agent Operations Coordination
```

**🔑 Key: UN Never Overrides National Decisions**
- UN provides *coordination and advisory services*
- Nations choose which UN services to use
- National courts always have final jurisdiction
- UN recommendations are non-binding by default
